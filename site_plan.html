Site Plan: Partido Boulevard

Site Purpose: Partido Boulevard

The two-kilometer completed portion of the coastal by-pass road in Barangay Tagas, San Jose, Camarines Sur, Philippines has already become a tourist attraction among local residents and tourists from neighboring towns and places in the province. The road project officially called "Construction of By-pass and Diversion Road, Junction of Nato-Sagnay Road, Tagas Section, Barangay Tagas, San Jose, Camarines Sur Philippines is being implemented by the Camarines Sur 4th District Engineering Office of the Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH). Town officials are optimistic that the boulevard will open business opportunities in the area, such as beach resorts, hotels, restaurants, souvenir stores, and other tourism-related shops.
